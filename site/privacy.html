Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2025

Overview

LittleHood ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information in compliance with GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and applicable data protection laws.

Information We Do NOT Collect

LittleHood does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. We have:

  • No user accounts
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertisements
  • No external data collection
  • No cookies or similar technologies

Information Stored Locally

The following information is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Your name and profile photo (optional)
  • Training sessions and scores
  • Equipment configurations
  • Location data (only if you enable location services)
  • Weather conditions (retrieved via Apple Weather)

Legal Basis for Processing

Under GDPR Article 6, the legal basis for processing your data is:

  • Consent: You explicitly consent to data processing when you enter information or enable features
  • Legitimate Interest: Processing is necessary for the app's core functionality

Location Services

LittleHood uses location services to:

  • Record the location of your training sessions
  • Provide accurate weather data for your training location
  • Display training locations on maps within the app

Important: Location tracking is completely optional. You can:

  • Deny location permissions when prompted
  • Disable location services anytime in iOS Settings
  • Use the app fully without location features

Location data is stored only on your device and in your personal iCloud account if sync is enabled.

Third-Party Services

Apple Weather

LittleHood integrates with Apple Weather (WeatherKit) to provide weather conditions during your training sessions. When you use this feature:

  • Your location is sent to Apple's servers to retrieve weather data
  • Apple processes this request according to their Privacy Policy
  • We do not have access to any data Apple collects
  • Weather data is stored locally on your device

For more information, see Apple's Privacy Policy

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync:

  • Your data syncs between your own devices using Apple's CloudKit
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Only you can access your iCloud data
  • We have no access to your iCloud account or data

Data Storage and Security

Your data is protected through:

  • iOS device encryption
  • Secure iCloud encryption (if enabled)
  • No external server communication
  • Local-only data processing

Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your data:

Right How to Exercise
Access View all your data directly in the app
Rectification Edit or update your data anytime in the app
Erasure Delete individual records or remove the app entirely
Portability Export your data using the app's export feature
Restriction Disable features like location or weather tracking
Objection Opt out of any feature at any time

Data Retention

Your data is retained:

  • As long as the app is installed on your device
  • In iCloud if sync is enabled (until you delete it)
  • You can delete all data by removing the app

International Data Transfers

When using Apple Weather:

  • Location data may be processed by Apple servers globally
  • Apple ensures appropriate safeguards for international transfers
  • All other data remains on your device or in your iCloud account

Children's Privacy

LittleHood is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app functions the same for all users regardless of age.
